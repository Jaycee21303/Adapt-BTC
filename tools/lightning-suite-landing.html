Why We Built This

Bitcoin adoption can be overwhelming, especially for beginners and small businesses. The Lightning Network adds even more complexity — channels, liquidity, invoices, routing, fees, node selection, and payment failures.

The AdaptBTC Lightning Adoption Suite solves this with an integrated, zero-cost, browser-based tool that educates, analyzes, and guides users step-by-step.

FAQ

Do I need an account?

No. Everything runs client-side in the browser. You can replace demo data with your own endpoints when ready.

Will it hit our node?

Only if you point safeFetch to your node. The default build uses public data so workshops remain safe.

Can I export reports?

Yes—take screenshots, copy JSON, or wire a simple download button for uptime logs and drill summaries.

Does it cost anything?

No. It is published as a public good. We encourage forks, translations, and local customizations.

Where do I start?

Launch the suite, click “Generate Route,” and load the rollout planner demo. You can plug in your own numbers later.
