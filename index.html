Why Bitcoin Matters

Inflation Protection

Bitcoin has a fixed supply of 21 million, giving it predictable monetary properties unlike inflationary fiat currency.

True Ownership

You control your money directly. No bank can freeze, reverse, or limit your transactions.

Borderless Payments

Send value anywhere in the world — instantly, securely, and without middlemen.

Most Secure Network

Bitcoin is secured by the largest decentralized computing network ever created.
