Launch packages

Team playbooks ready to download

Choose a profile and we’ll generate the right mix of custody drills, vendor steps, and success metrics. These mirror the Lightning suite controls and can be copied into your wiki.

Copy/paste friendly

Retail rollout

  • 2–3 channels per lane with one hosted fallback.
  • Staff SOP: QR + NFC, offline QR fallback, and refunds.
  • Weekly drills: stuck HTLC, device lost/stolen, payout audit.

Services + invoices

  • Invoices tagged with project IDs for reconciliation.
  • 2-of-3 signer chart with rotation and patching cadence.
  • Uptime probes every 10 minutes during business hours.

Nonprofit & grants

  • Static donation QR plus variable invoices for campaigns.
  • Monthly transparency export of BTC kept vs converted.
  • Dual-control for cold storage and travel wallets.

Investor talking points

  • Publish fee savings vs card processors and uptime trendlines.
  • Show routing diversity: at least three high-uptime peers.
  • List quarterly drills completed and action items closed.

Team commitments

  • Rotate signing devices every six months with firmware checks.
  • Keep a hot/hybrid/hosted fallback plan documented.
  • Tag every incident and drill in an audit-friendly log.

Workshop-in-a-box

Teach teams how to use the tools in one afternoon

If you need to showcase the suite for investors or a board review, run this two-hour agenda. Everything is copy/paste ready—slides, prompts, and checkpoints.

120-minute flow

Hour 1: Foundations

  • Walk through the homepage price ticker and halving clock.
  • Load DCA demo inputs and discuss discipline vs speculation.
  • Introduce Lightning vs card fee savings and hardware payback.

Hour 2: Operations

  • Open the Lightning suite and explore live mempool + routing panels.
  • Generate a rollout plan with the planner and copy outputs to slides.
  • Run a drill: lost device + stuck HTLC recovery using the checklists.

Follow-ups

  • Assign owners for uptime probes and channel rebalancing SOPs.
  • Schedule quarterly reviews; export screenshots for the data room.
  • Translate the playbooks for local teams and share via community page.

Slide prompts

  • “What does Lightning solve for our customers today?”
  • “How much margin do we keep vs card processors?”
  • “Which drills prove we’re ready for outages?”
  • “What’s our signer rotation and firmware cadence?”

Success metrics

  • 99%+ uptime and <2% failed invoices with documented retries.
  • Clear payback math for any hardware purchases.
  • Every signer has completed a restore test in the last 90 days.
  • Drill logs live in a shared folder with owners and dates.
