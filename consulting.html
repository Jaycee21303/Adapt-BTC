How we work together

  1. Discovery call (15 minutes) — your goals, risk tolerance, and what “good” looks like for you or your team.
  2. Plan build-out — we craft a session plan, tools list, and homework so live time is efficient.
  3. Live sessions — screens-on, step-by-step execution with clear checkpoints and recorded action items.
  4. Security review — backup validation, withdrawal test, and stress scenarios to confirm confidence.
  5. Follow-up — 30-day Q&A window via email plus optional refresher session if you want us to double-check changes.
