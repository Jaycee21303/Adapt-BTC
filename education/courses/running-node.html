Running a Node

Node roles, hardware options, privacy, and data integrity.

Key concepts

Education and operations go hand in hand for business rollouts.

  • Design training around decision trees: custody, geography, customer type.
  • Teach escalation paths for stuck transactions and channel issues.
  • Align accounting and product teams on invoice data models.
  • Run post-mortems for fee spikes and liquidity drains.

Implementation guidance

Your payment experience must work offline and degrade gracefully.

  • Cache critical data like exchange rates and fee policies.
  • Offer fallback on-chain payments when Lightning liquidity is constrained.
  • Plan monitoring for channel balances and HTLC failures.
  • Align customer support scripts with incident policies.
