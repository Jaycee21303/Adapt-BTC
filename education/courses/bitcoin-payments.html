Bitcoin Payments

POS integrations, refunds, settlements, and reconciliation.

Key concepts

Consensus needs reliable hardware, clean storage, and observability.

  • Run nodes on SSDs with ECC RAM when possible.
  • Use checksum verification for client binaries.
  • Enable logging and backups of channel.db for Lightning.
  • Prefer Tor or VPN for privacy and resilience.

Implementation guidance

Shipping Bitcoin features requires testing environments that mirror production.

  • Mirror your production topology with regtest and signet.
  • Track fee estimation inputs when building wallets.
  • Use PSBT flows to support multi-party signing.
  • Automate backups and verify restores quarterly.
