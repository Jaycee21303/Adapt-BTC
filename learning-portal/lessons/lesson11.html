Treasury Strategy

Businesses treat bitcoin as strategic treasury reserve and collateral.

Saylor strategy

Accumulate BTC as a long-term treasury asset, funded responsibly.

Borrow against BTC

Collateralized loans provide liquidity without selling holdings.

Long-term holding

Align incentives around multi-year horizons and custody discipline.

Treasury planning

Define allocation limits, rebalancing rules, and board oversight.

Summary

  • Treat BTC as strategic reserve with governance.
  • Consider collateralized borrowing carefully.
  • Document policies, limits, and signers.
