Lesson 12

Bitcoin for Business

Operationalizing Bitcoin requires clear custody models, policies, and awareness of regulations.

Business custody

Choose between self-custody, collaborative custody, or qualified custodians based on capability.

Enterprise wallets

Role-based access, policy engines, and audit logs support internal controls.

Multi-sig

Threshold signatures distribute control and reduce single points of failure.

Regulations basics

Understand KYC/AML obligations, travel rule considerations, and tax implications.

Policies

Document onboarding, backups, signer rotation, and incident response.

Summary

  • Pick custody that fits skills and regulation.
  • Use multi-sig or policy engines for defense-in-depth.
  • Train staff and document procedures.
