Lesson 10

Bitcoin Privacy

Privacy requires deliberate UTXO management and good operational hygiene.

Address reuse

Reusing addresses links transactions. Always generate fresh addresses.

UTXO management

Spending patterns can reveal balances. Consolidate when fees are low and avoid merging unrelated UTXOs.

Coin control

Choose specific UTXOs when spending to avoid linking histories.

Lightning privacy

Lightning adds hop-level privacy but channel openings/closures are on-chain.

Summary

  • Use new addresses for every receive.
  • Manage UTXOs intentionally with coin control.
  • Lightning helps obscure payment flows but anchors are public.
