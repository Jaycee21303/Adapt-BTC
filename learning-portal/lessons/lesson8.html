Transactions & Fees

Transaction design and fee selection influence confirmation speed.

Mempool

The mempool holds unconfirmed transactions prioritized by fee rate.

RBF

Replace-By-Fee allows increasing a transaction’s fee before confirmation.

SegWit

Segregated Witness reduces weight and mitigates malleability.

Taproot

Taproot improves privacy and flexibility by aggregating spending conditions.

Priority

Higher fee rates typically confirm faster; fee estimators help choose tiers.

Summary

  • Fee markets respond to blockspace demand.
  • SegWit and Taproot reduce weight and improve privacy.
  • RBF provides flexibility when fees spike.
