On-chain vs Lightning

Bitcoin settles on-chain; Lightning enables instant payments using channels.

Settlement

On-chain transactions finalize on the blockchain. Lightning settles off-chain with eventual on-chain anchors.

Channels

Lightning channels lock funds in multi-signature outputs, enabling peer-to-peer payment pathways.

Routing

Payments hop through nodes with available liquidity, guided by routing algorithms.

Speed

Lightning payments confirm in milliseconds, ideal for point-of-sale or API use.

Fees

On-chain fees depend on blockspace demand; Lightning fees are typically low but depend on liquidity paths.

Summary

  • On-chain = global settlement; Lightning = instant small payments.
  • Channels require inbound and outbound liquidity.
  • Route selection balances reliability and fees.
