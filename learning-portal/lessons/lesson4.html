Self-Custody

Self-custody means controlling your private keys so no intermediary can block access to your bitcoin.

Keys

A private key signs transactions; a public key and address receive funds.

Seed phrase

A 12–24 word phrase encodes the master key for deterministic wallets.

Wallets

Software or hardware that store keys, build transactions, and display balances.

Recovery

Secure backups of the seed phrase allow restoration on any compatible wallet.

Custodial risk

Exchanges can freeze accounts. Self-custody reduces counterparty and jurisdictional risk.

Summary

  • Control the keys, control the coins.
  • Protect the seed phrase from loss and disclosure.
  • Favor open-source wallets and backups.
