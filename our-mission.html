Our Mission

AdaptBTC exists to make Bitcoin education accessible, practical, and empowering for the everyday person — not just experts, traders, or institutions. We help people take responsible custody of their money, understand the risks and rewards, and build financial resilience for the long term.

Our Vision

Become the most accessible, practical, and trustworthy Bitcoin onboarding and guidance hub for everyday people, small businesses, and treasury managers.

We want to bridge the gap between traditional financial thinking and the emerging world of decentralized digital money — without hype, fear, or confusion.

Mission in one line

Equip everyday people to self-custody and use Bitcoin safely.

North star metric

People who complete onboarding, secure backups, and withdraw from exchanges.

Why AdaptBTC Exists

Bitcoin is powerful — but the learning curve is intimidating for most new people. Between misinformation, scams, hype, and technical jargon, it’s easy to feel lost.

Our goal is to create a safe, trustworthy, beginner-friendly environment where anyone can get started the right way and build lasting confidence.

Whether you're an individual or a small business exploring Bitcoin for the first time, AdaptBTC gives you the education and clarity you need to move forward.

Impact We're Aiming For

  • 10,000 newcomers completing a secure self-custody setup with backups.
  • 500+ small businesses trained on treasury basics and incident preparedness.
  • Zero tolerance for hype: every recommendation is vetted, practical, and risk-aware.
  • Global accessibility: guides translated, jargon removed, examples localized.

Our Commitments

  • Education over speculation: we never promise price outcomes.
  • Security first: self-custody, backups, and recovery are taught before buying more.
  • Open resources: core guides remain freely accessible.
  • Evidence-based: we cite data, research, and real-world examples for every claim.
