Join the Bitcoin Community

Here are four simple paths to begin participating in the global Bitcoin movement:

1. Learn from Trusted Sources

These are reputable, high-quality Bitcoin education organizations and sources:

  • Bitcoin.org
  • Bitcoin Magazine
  • Swan Bitcoin Education
  • Mempool Explorer

2. Join the Community

Engage with others who are learning and building in the Bitcoin space:

  • Stacker News
  • Bitcoin community on X
  • r/Bitcoin
  • Bitcoin meetups & dev groups

3. Explore Bitcoin Tools

These tools help you interact with Bitcoin and learn deeper concepts:

  • BlueWallet (Beginner Wallet)
  • Phoenix Wallet (Lightning)
  • Trezor (Hardware Wallet)
  • Coldcard (Advanced Security)
