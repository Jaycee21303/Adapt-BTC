How Lightning donations work

Lightning payments are near-instant and low-fee. Use any Lightning-enabled wallet (Cash App, Phoenix, Muun, Wallet of Satoshi, etc.) to send support.

1) Open your wallet

Choose “Send” or “Pay invoice.”

2) Scan or paste

Scan the QR or paste the invoice string above.

3) Confirm & send

Select an amount, confirm, and your support is delivered.
